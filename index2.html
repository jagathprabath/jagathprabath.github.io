Welcome to Princeway College.


Princeway College is a private English Medium School which mainly forces on National Syllabus to the children irrespective of Gender in Avissawella area and surrounding towns. It is a Primary School which provides education to the children in the level of Kindergarten to Grade 5 at the beginning and will be extended to GCE Ordinary and Advance Level in future. It also conducts private Tuition classes for Local Syllabus from Grade1 up to GCE (AL) for English Medium and Sinhala Medium students during weekends and week days after school hours. In addition, it conducts private Tuition classes for London Cambridge syllabus. It always commits to Lifelong Learning in a Caring Environment and dedicates to excellence of child education. Furthermore, a learning community inspires to building respectful and responsible citizens and empowering all learners. Students maximize potential through individual attention of resource personnel and they discover new learning possibilities. Moreover, it provides education through motivation and inspiration and it formulates a community with high expectation and high academic achievement. It also inspiring children for exciting futures. Working collaboratively to ensure every student achieves academically, socially, and emotionally.
