School Values

We, the Teachers and staff of the Princeway College commit to the following values:

  • Leadership: We value and are committed to exercising leadership characterized by innovation and vision.
  • Excellence: We value dedication to excellence in teaching and service. We are committed to promoting creative and critical thinking among teachers, students and staff within a culture of inspiration, high expectations, accountability and quality service to students and external communities.
  • Social and Ethical Responsibility: We value honesty, fairness, respect, compassion, and professional and scholarly ethics among staff, teachers and students. We are committed to increasing access to opportunity and promoting social justice.
  • Diversity: We value respect for cultural, individual and role difference, including those due to age, gender, race, ethnicity, national origin, religion, sexual orientation, marital status, family configuration, disability, language, philosophical orientation, and socioeconomic status among others. We are committed to continuous critical reflections of self and practice central to the true spirit of diversity.
  • Communication and Collaboration: We value open communication and collaboration with Staff and our external communities. We are committed to maintaining a spirit of community where differences of opinion are encouraged.
  • Customer orientation: We value customers such as students, parents and other communities in which render services to their maximum satisfaction by way of gathering data through feedback surveys and providing solutions for any criticisms.
